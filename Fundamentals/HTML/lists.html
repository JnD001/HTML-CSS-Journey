Mythical Creatures

Dragon
A legendary, powerful creature often depicted as a large, winged reptile capable of breathing fire. Dragons are symbols of strength, wisdom, and magic in many cultures.
Phoenix
A mythical bird associated with fire and rebirth. It is said to burst into flames at the end of its life and rise anew from its ashes, symbolizing immortality and renewal.
Vampire
A supernatural being that feeds on the blood of the living to sustain itself. Vampires are often portrayed as immortal, pale, and nocturnal creatures with fangs, symbolizing seduction and danger.
Werewolf
A human who transforms into a wolf or wolf-like creature, often during a full moon. Werewolves are associated with uncontrollable rage, primal instincts, and the duality of human nature.
